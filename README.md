# His Honor, the Buckaroo

A biographical website dedicated to **Mayor John L. Davie** (1850–1934), Oakland's longest-serving and most colorful mayor.

Mule driver. Opera singer. Rancher. Attorney. Railroad fighter. Five-term mayor.

## About

John Leslie Davie was born in upstate New York in 1850 and lived one of the most improbable lives in California history. This site tells his story — from the Erie Canal to the Oakland waterfront wars to his 18 years as mayor.
